About Yoga

Movements, breathing exercises, guided meditations, and relaxation techniques are just some of the tools that yoga has to offer. Yoga is a way to maintain health and well-being in a stressful society but also a way to increase self-awareness and transformation.

Practicing yoga has many benefits for mental and physical health. More and more scientific research is done on yoga, which means that we now have scientific support for most of the benefits. Here are some of them:

  • Reduces stress, anxiety, tension and promotes relaxation
  • Reduces inflammation
  • Improves sleep quality
  • Antidepressant effect
  • Relief of chronic pain
  • Improves flexibility and physical balance
  • Deeper and slower breathing
  • Promotes healthy eating habits
  • Increases blood circulation
  • Increases immune system
  • Lowers high blood pressure
  • Lowers blood sugar
  • Calming effect on the nervous system
  • Increases ability to concentrate
  • Positive effect on the digestive system
  • Gives you inner strength and builds awareness

About Michelle

I am a dedicated yoga instructor, wellness enthusiast, and devoted mamma of two. However, I wasn't always a yogi and wellness devotee. Luckily I was introduced to yoga by another curious soul in 2011. The practice of yoga quickly began benefiting me, and I experienced a profound shift inside of my mind, body and spirit. As my passion grew, I felt compelled to share my newfound knowledge and skill so that others might experience positive results in their own lives. Today, I continue to be a curious seeker, always striving to learn more... and more!

Yoga is not just doing a few poses once you've rolled out your mat. It is a way of life. It encompasses a variety of practices you can do sitting quietly at your desk or even when teaching a child a life skill. Yoga is a practice of love that welcomes us to turn inward while seeking connections with others, as well as ourselves. I invite you to practice with me... to begin your own journey.

Classes We Offer

Body&Mind Yoga for Everyone

A yoga practice that combines movement and exercise for body and mind, promoting physical and mental health. This class is for everyone. The focus is on strength, balance, presence and of course relaxation. We end with Yoga Nidra or a guided relaxation. A class without requirements.

Personalised Yoga Sessions

The therapeutic effect of an individual session is aimed at restoring and maintaining inner balance, managing, and preventing ill health. All yoga components with which we practice together are carefully selected to work in a practical and targeted manner and are adapted to meet your personal needs.

Prenatal/Pregnancy Yoga

During this course and every Tuesday we will do beautiful yoga movements that are adapted to the pregnant body, strengthen, soften and relieve tension. We will also go through and practice different types of pranayama (breathing techniques. The course is adapted to the participants, which means that you are welcome in all weeks ( we recommend second and third trimester).
